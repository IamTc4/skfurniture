About skfurniture

Building the Foundation of Learning Since 2010

Who We Are

skfurniture is a leading manufacturer of educational and institutional furniture based in Thane, Maharashtra. We specialize in creating ergonomic, durable, and aesthetically pleasing furniture solutions for schools, colleges, and offices.

With a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and a commitment to BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) guidelines, we ensure that every desk, chair, and table we produce contributes to a healthy learning environment. Our journey began with a simple mission: to replace outdated, uncomfortable classrooms with modern, inspiring spaces.

Today, we serve over 100+ institutions across Mumbai, Thane, and Pune, delivering excellence through precision engineering and customer-centric service.

Our Mission

To revolutionize educational infrastructure in India by providing high-quality, safe, and ergonomic furniture that enhances student focus and well-being.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted furniture partner for educational institutions across Western India, known for innovation, durability, and sustainable manufacturing.
