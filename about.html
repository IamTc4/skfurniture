Who We Are

Sai Krupa Furniture & Fabrication Works (also known as S.K. Furniture & Fabrication Works) has been highly recognized as a trusted manufacturer of:

  • School & college benches
  • Classroom benches
  • School & college furniture
  • Green boards, notice boards, whiteboards
  • Tables, chairs, partitions, and more

Our Approach

We manufacture benches in various attractive shades, tailored to classroom size and wall color, making them both visually appealing and comfortable for students. Our designs help maximize classroom capacity and comfort.

We have a tendency to style our merchandise to surpass expectations in sophistication, practicality, and aesthetics.

Quality & Recognition

Our products are factory-made using the finest grade materials and are designed with high precision to meet commercial quality standards. Our offerings are highly appreciated by customers for their attractive appearance, sturdy construction, and long service life.
