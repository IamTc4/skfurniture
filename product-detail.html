Designed for the Modern Classroom

The Standard Dual Bench by skfurniture is engineered to withstand the rigors of daily classroom use while providing maximum comfort. Unlike standard market furniture, our benches focus on spinal health.

Why Choose CRCA Steel? Cold Rolled Close Annealed steel offers superior strength and surface finish compared to mild steel. Our 7-tank process ensures the powder coating adheres perfectly, preventing rust even in humid conditions.
