Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide bulk discounts for schools?

Yes, we specialize in B2B supply. We offer tiered pricing based on order volume. Please contact us for a custom quote.

Are your products BIS compliant?

Absolutely. All our classroom furniture follows IS 4837 standards for dimensions and ergonomics to ensure student health and safety.

What is the warranty period?

We offer a standard 5-year warranty on all structural steel components and a 1-year warranty on wooden parts against manufacturing defects.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, our team handles delivery and professional installation at your site to ensure everything is set up correctly.
