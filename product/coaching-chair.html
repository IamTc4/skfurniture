About this item

  • Heavy-Duty Construction: Built with 16-gauge (1.5mm) CRCA round/square steel pipes, ensuring superior load-bearing capacity and stability.
  • Premium Wood Finish: 18mm/25mm thick Action Tesa Pre-Laminated Board (MDF/PLB) with 2mm PVC Edge Banding for moisture resistance and smooth edges.
  • 7-Tank Anti-Rust Treatment: Steel frames undergo rigorous chemical degreasing, phosphating, and passivation before powder coating.
  • Safety & Ergonomics: Zero-sharp edges with injection-molded caps and anthropometrically designed seat angles to reduce student fatigue.
  • Floor Protection: Equipped with heavy-duty nylon levelers/bushings to prevent floor scratches and reduce noise.

Premium Quality Dual Desk for Schools & Colleges

The Coaching Chair by skfurniture is engineered to withstand the rigors of daily institutional use while providing maximum comfort. Designed with ergonomics in mind, it supports modern learning and working environments.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Ergonomic Comfort: The desk height and seat inclination are compliant with IS 4837:1990 standards, ensuring students maintain a healthy posture, reducing back strain during extended lectures.
  • Superior Durability: The metal frame is MIG welded for seamless joints and coated with Epoxy Polyester Powder (60-70 microns), offering exceptional resistance to scratches, chipping, and corrosion.
  • Smart Storage: Includes a spacious integrated wire mesh or sheet metal shelf below the desk top, allowing students to store bags and books clutter-free.
  • Child-Safe Design: All corners are rounded or capped with high-quality ABS/Nylon buffers to prevent accidental injuries in active classroom environments.

Application Areas

Perfect for Primary Schools, High Schools, University Lecture Halls, Training Centers, and Computer Labs.
